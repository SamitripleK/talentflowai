Job description

About the role

Aperture Labs is looking for a Senior Product Designer to shape the next generation of our AI-driven products. You'll own end-to-end design for core product areas — from early discovery and research through polished, shipped experiences used by hundreds of thousands of people.

Day-to-day, you'll partner closely with product managers and engineers to turn ambiguous problems into clear, elegant solutions. You'll run research sessions, sketch and prototype ideas quickly, contribute to and extend our design system, and pressure-test flows with real users before they ship.

Expected outcomes: within your first 90 days you'll have shipped at least one meaningful product improvement, established a rhythm of continuous user research, and helped raise the quality bar across the design team through critique and shared components.

Key responsibilities

What you'll do

Design user-centered interfaces that balance business goals with real user needs.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams across product, engineering, and research.
Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions.
Build and maintain scalable design systems and component libraries.
Create interactive prototypes to communicate ideas and de-risk decisions early.
Mentor designers and elevate craft through critique and documentation.
